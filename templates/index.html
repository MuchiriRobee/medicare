    About Us

    Welcome to HealthPoint Medical Clinic

    Established in 2005, HealthPoint has been providing exceptional healthcare services to our community for over 20 years. Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with the latest medical technology to ensure accurate diagnosis and effective treatment.

    Our team consists of board-certified physicians, experienced nurses, and friendly support staff who are committed to providing personalized care in a comfortable environment. We believe in treating the whole person, not just the symptoms.
